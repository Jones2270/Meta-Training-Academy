Level 2 Award in Safe Moving and Handling


INTRODUCTION


Ensure your team keep themselves safe when moving and handling! The majority of accidents resulting from moving and handling issues can be avoided with the proper training and knowledge. The Meta Training Academy Level 2 Award in Safe Moving and Handling has been designed for learners/employees who need specific training around moving and handling, or for refresher training.


It aims to ensure learners/employees understand that moving and handling must be carried out the right way, and that they recognise its importance in contributing to a safe working environment


Topics covered include:


  • Understanding the reasons for safe manual handling
  • Understanding how manual handling risk assessments contribute to improving health and safety
  • Understanding the principles, types of equipment and testing requirements associated with manual handling safety
  • Applying safe manual handling principles

Assessment

MCQ exam and Practical Examination


ENTRY REQUIREMENTS


There are no specific entry requirements.


Career Progression


The objective of this qualification is to prepare learners to progress to a qualification in another subject area and/or give learners personal growth and engagement in learning, especially in relation to understanding the basic principles of safe moving and handling. This qualification can be taken by any learners preparing to enter employment or by those who are already in employment and wish to develop their knowledge of safe moving and handling.
